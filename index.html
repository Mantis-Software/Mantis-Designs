— FAQ

Frequently asked
question

What types of projects do you undertake?
We work with various mediums such as 3D printing, woodworking, software, apparel, and electronics to produce unique, tailored creations. Contact us to see if we would be the best fit for your project.
How involved are clients in the design process?
Clients are involved at every step, from concept to delivery. From planning to delivery we will update our clients with progress messages and images.
What makes your studio unique?
We blend digital and physical mediums to craft one-of-a-kind pieces. By combining materials and techniques across disciplines, we create designs that wouldn’t be possible through a single form of creation alone.
What’s your typical project timeline?
It depends on complexity, but most are completed within 2-4 weeks.
