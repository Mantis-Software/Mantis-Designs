FAQ

What finishes do you use?

We use a range of film and penetrating finishes depending on use‑case: hardwax‑oil for a natural feel, waterborne polyurethane for durable low‑odor protection, and oil‑based finishes for high‑wear situations.

Can you match my existing kitchen or furniture color?

Yes—we create test swatches and adjust dye/stain and finish sheen until we’re aligned.

How do custom projects work?

We start with a quick call, gather measurements and inspiration, then provide a concept, timeline, and estimate. After approval, we build and finish, then coordinate delivery or install.
