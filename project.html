How AI is Changing Craftsmanship

By Mantis Designs • June 6, 2025

In the age of automation, the creative industries are undergoing a renaissance. From 3D modeling software powered by AI suggestions to CNC machines interpreting hand-drawn concepts, artisans are blending tradition with technology.

At Mantis Designs, we embrace this balance. Our 3D designs begin with intuition and are enhanced through generative techniques to reduce waste and optimize structure. Yet, every product still passes through the hands of a human designer who ensures beauty and integrity.

AI isn't replacing creativity — it’s augmenting it. It’s giving craftspeople new tools to push boundaries while staying rooted in thoughtful design.

As we look forward, the relationship between human and machine will only deepen — but the soul of the craft will always remain with the maker.
