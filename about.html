/ About Us /

Rooted in craft. Built for real life.

We’re a small, hands‑on studio creating durable wood pieces with clean lines and warm character. From patient refinishing to fully custom builds—and smart 3D‑print integrations where they make sense—we design for everyday use, not just showrooms.

Our Story

Mantis Designs started in Houston, Texas with a simple belief: the objects you live with should work, feel good, and last. We combine traditional joinery with modern tooling - we pair some designs with 3D printing to elevate the product and deliver elegance to our customers.

Most projects begin with a conversation and a sketch. We visit spaces, take measurements, test finishes, and source hardwoods thoughtfully. The result is a piece that feels like it has always belonged in your home.

Today, we proudly serve Houston, Austin, San Antonio, and Dallas—bringing custom craft and clever design to homes across Texas.

Honest Materials
