Custom Wood Wall Panel Decor

We design and build custom wood wall panel decor tailored to you and your home. Unlike the current mass produced wood wall panels, we elevate our designs by using the best quality wood with different colors along with unique designs.

Acoustic Wall Panel

One of our clients wanted to have a professional grade acoustic wall, so we aimed to have the best quality materials with professional grade acoustic insulation.

* Acoustic wall frame to support wooden strips and house the acoustic insulation.


* Denim insulation used for the sound absorbtion. This insulation also does not irritate the skin as it is made from recycled denim from clothes.


* A breathable black fabric was used to allow for sound absorbtion along with fitting with the color scheme the clients wanted.
