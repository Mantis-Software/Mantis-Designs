Modded Pokemon Go Plus +

By Mantis Designs • December 17, 2024

At Mantis Designs, we specialize in custom electronics and 3D printing solutions.

This project was created as a Christmas gift to improve the original Pokémon Go Plus + device, which vibrates constantly during use. While many users simply cut the wire to disable the motor, we took a more thoughtful approach by adding a switch that allows the user to easily turn the vibration on or off.

We also designed and printed a new case, as the original housing didn’t have room for the added switch. The custom case is deeper, allowing it to accommodate both the existing electronics and the new feature seamlessly.

If you're interested in custom electronics modifications or personalized 3D-printed projects, feel free to reach out—we’d be happy to help bring your idea to life.
