In today's day in age, we are constantly bombarded with work of corporations which maximize profits with little care in the user experience and the longevity of the product. With the rise of AI, we are starting to see this behavior bleed into media for enterntainment and professional uses. Art and products made for humans should be beautiful with the human user in mind. Our goal at Mantis Designs is to provide quality product that maximizes beauty and function over profits - aiming for our pieces to be relied on for lifetimes and beyond.
